Mission

  • Build Relationships: We support and represent our artists in every way, shape and form.
  • Bridge Partnerships: We work with our artists cohesively as a unified front to turn dreams into reality.
  • Creative & Safe Environment: Feeling safe is critical if the artistry is to thrive. We create an environment where this can happen.
  • Mentorship: We provide mentoring to further develop a strong sense of artist identity.
  • Recognize Obstacles: We understand that obstacles are present, but together strive to ensure our artists persevere toward greatness.

About

Sara Yazdani Furrer

Sara Yazdani Furrer began her career in entertainment public relations and then seamlessly transitioned to artist management. With a love for music, Sara applied her passion and drive towards representing artists that didn’t have a voice in this ever-changing music industry. With proven results and experience, Sara officially launched YD Management to bring her mission to life.

Liz Labelle

Liz Labelle is a Montreal-based pop singer-songwriter and instrumentalist. Her passion took her on the road following the 2014 Vans Warped Tour to promote her debut full-length record Tanqueray Dreams. Vans Warped Tour founder and CEO, Kevin Lyman, noticed Liz’s drive and pure dedication and awarded her four impromptu shows on Warped Tour: Portland, Atlanta, Nashville and Milwaukee. Other career highlights include: a U.S. tour opening for Rookie Of The Year wrapping in Los Angeles, placing tracks in Nickelodeon’s movie Naomi And Ely’s No Kiss List, collaborating with EDM artists, Ampera & Tryple, on the popular track “Rebellion” and winning second place at the "Unsigned Only Songwriting Competition.” You can find her album Tanqueray Dreams on iTunes, and Liz is currently in the studio working on her next album.
